Inter School

Quiz Contest

Sony BBC Earth gives you an opportunity to make
your school proud and showcase your talent to the world.

For the FIRST TIME EVER,
we’re excited to launch the Inter school Quiz Competition
for students from Std 5th – Std 9th

Pair up in teams of two (Both students to be from the same school and same standard):

step 01

Pair up in teams of two and register by entering your details by going to the main page

step 02

Enter your team member’s email id, if not already done. A common CODE will be generated and shared with you and your team member

Enter the CODE to begin the quiz and answer the questions

step 03

The team that answers majority of the questions right will stand a chance to qualify to the next round

In case there is a tie between two teams, the team that has answered in the least amount of time will qualify

While the test will be taken by individual team members, the final score will be a total of both the members in the same team

How to Play

Select one answer from the 4 options given. After finishing quiz your score will be displayed (the second member will see the total team score3). Each time can only participate once
